Designed for reading and exploring

Open Bible was created to read like a book. Choose a theme to match your lighting and enjoy reading without hurting your eyes.

Explore the land of the Bible and show events on a map for each book of the Bible.

Built by Developers, for Developers

Built on the modules provided by Sword Project.

Open Bible is open source, which means you can take a look under the hood and contribute.
