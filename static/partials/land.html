Open Bible


A modern app designed for reading and exploring the Bible.

Open Bible was built to read like a book. Choose a theme to match your lighting and enjoy longform reading without hurting your eyes. The typography was carefully selected to provide the optimal reading experience.

Explore the land of the Bible and show events on a map.

Crafted by developers from GitHub.

Built on the modules provided by Sword Project.

Open Bible is open source, which means you can take a look under the hood and contribute.
